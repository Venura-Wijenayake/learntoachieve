L2A v1.0 — Site relaunch + LinkedIn reclaim

Learn to Achieve relaunched today after a period of organizational dormancy. The site you're reading lives at l2a.me — a bridge domain we'll use until budget allows a proper .org acquisition.

What's now live:

  • Static site at l2a.me with full brand system, JSON-LD structured data, social-share OG image, and SEO hygiene
  • LinkedIn company page reclaimed and populated with current programs, About copy, and specialties
  • Flagship project — the U.S. Healthcare Expenditure Explorer — re-anchored under the L2A brand. 28,255 CMS Medicare Part D records currently queryable, covering Q1 2024 through Q2 2025

Compliance posture: We are in active remediation with federal and California state authorities — a routine cleanup following the 2021–2025 organizational dormancy. We expect to restore full 501(c)(3) standing within 6–8 weeks under IRS Rev. Proc. 2014-11 streamlined reinstatement. Until then, we publish under our California public-benefit nonprofit corporate registration (Entity #C4084984).

Next up: A California Healthcare Workforce GIS dashboard, currently in scoping with partners at the California Department of Workforce Development. More open-source civic-data tools in the pipeline. Future updates will be posted here as work ships.
